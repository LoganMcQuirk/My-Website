ABOUT ME

Hi, I’m Logan, a versatile creative with a background in graphic design, coding, and music. Over the past year, I’ve had the opportunity to explore three distinct yet interconnected areas of work:

  • Graphic Design: I’ve designed professional training materials and learning resources for Create Music, a leading peripatetic music teaching company, and developed artwork for music acts.
  • Web Development: I’m proficient in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and have built this portfolio site from scratch using my coding skills. I’m constantly expanding my knowledge through online projects and tutorials on platforms like GitHub and LinkedIn Learning.
  • Music Performance: As a bass guitarist, I perform with an agency functions band at weddings and live events, continuing my long-standing passion for music and live performance.

With a degree in Music Industry Practice from the Academy of Contemporary Music, I bring creativity, technical skill, and adaptability to everything I do. My diverse experiences—ranging from teaching and design to coding and live music—equip me with a unique skill set, making me eager to tackle new challenges in the digital and creative sectors.

I’m passionate about learning, and I’m always looking for ways to grow as a designer, developer, and musician. Feel free to explore my portfolio, and don’t hesitate to get in touch if you’d like to collaborate or discuss potential opportunities!
